Buy to bet a coin rises; Short to bet it falls. A short profits as the price drops (toward +100% near zero), but if the price climbs past your entry it goes negative — covering then costs you coins, so a short gone wrong can drain far more than you staked. Prices update every 30s and drift up over the day. ⚠️ Unpaid loans build market instability; at 100% a CRASH tanks a random handful of coins by random amounts — while one random coin spikes up (you never know which, or how hard), so blanket-shorting is never safe.
